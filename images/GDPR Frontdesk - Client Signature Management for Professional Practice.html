GDPR Frontdesk

The GDPR software for your practice
Background:

  • This application was originally developed to address the GDPR requirements for a medical practice.  


Problem:
  • A practitioner needs every patient to sign a list of questions stating they understand what personal data and why that data is required.  They are granting permission to use the data stated for the purpose stated. Conventionally this is done by printing a form ($cost) that they will sign and then will have to be stored and managed ($cost + $time). Over a relatively short period of time there will be a growing stack of boxes filled with signed forms that need to be indexed and securely stored. ($ecurity issues + $time + $cost)

Solution:
  • GDPR Frontdesk. A simple application that allows creation of questionnaires for any purpose. These are presented to the client on a tablet device for signing. The output is a password protected PDF file that can then be saved against the client's account on the practice professional software of choice.

Applications:
  • Medical Practice - Doctor's Office - Dental Surgery - Schools - Sports Organisations - ...anywhere with a front desk that collects personal data.

Client side

The GDPR Frontdesk application presents your client with a clean presentation of the questions one by one. At the last step they are asked to sign the tablet if they agree.

Business side

In general the receptionist simply inserts name of the signatory and clicks "Start Questionnaire". 

Start Questionnaire will send the questions to the tablet. The receptionist can follow what's happening on her screen too and override the tablet to assist if necessary.

For versatility and if multiple questionnaires exist the receptionist may select a different pre-prepared questionnaire. The list of questions will appear in the table below. If any particular question does not apply then it may be deselected and it will not be asked.

Secure Output

The application then outputs a password protected PDF file that can be printed or uploaded into the client's account on the practice professional software.

GDPR Frontdesk does not store any client data.
GDPR Frontdesk is developed in Dublin, Ireland by independent developers.
